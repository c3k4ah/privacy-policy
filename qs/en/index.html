Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in the Quit Smoking (or "qs") mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

Quit Smoking is designed to respect your privacy.

  • Local Data Only: All data you enter into the application (quit date, cigarette consumption, savings, badges) is stored locally on your device using Shared Preferences.
  • No Remote Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers. Your progress stays on your phone.

2. Notifications

The app uses local notifications to provide reminders and motivation. These notifications are scheduled and triggered entirely on your device and do not involve any remote tracking or external messaging services.

3. Third-Party Services

The application may use the following third-party libraries:

  • Flutter Framework: To build the user interface.
  • Lottie: To display animations.
  • Shared Preferences: To save your data locally.

None of these libraries collect personal information on our behalf.

4. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send you reminders and celebrate your progress.
  • Exact Alarms: To ensure reminders are sent precisely at the time you choose.
  • Internet: Although not currently used for data transmission, this permission is included for potential future updates or essential system services.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact the developer.
